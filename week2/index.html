My name is Kaila Imada and I enjoy training stunts and martial arts in LA. I am finishing my undergraduate this summer before starting my studies in Entrepreneurship at USC in the fall. Before the summer ends, one of my goals is to travel and record my family history through interviews with my great uncle in Hiroshima, my grandfather in Oregon, and great aunt here in California. While I've set out with this goal, I realize now how much digital map making could be helpful with recording this process. I hope to learn the coding and digital map creation skills to enhance the user ability to access family records. Additionally, I hope to work with Keiya to create a map to record data for Japanese American memory (specific data TBD).
